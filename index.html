~ For Immediate Sale ~

This single-owner boat has been lovingly sailed and consistently maintained by a single owner. Fresh off a major refit, owners are moving to a slightly larger boat to continue their cruising adventures.

In the past two years, the boat has been fitted with all new electronics, a new generator, all new running rigging, and many other small repairs. So much of this boat is brand new!

The boat is located in the New Orleans area. You must see to believe.

History

  • Purchased new by family of four. Used in regular day sails and summer trips by family.
  • Never in charter service.
  • Clean, safe vessel, with proven track record in heavy seas, light air, and all conditions in between.
